# Hawassa City One Stop Center (Mesob Center) Project – Documentation Hub

This repository gathers drafts, reference documents, and collaborative materials focused on the planning, development, and ongoing activities of the Hawassa City One Stop Center (“Mesob Center”) initiative.

## Contents

- Drafts and versions of contracts, official documents, and meeting notes (primarily in `4_Feb_2026`)
- Collection of supporting reports, translations, and government communications
- Versioned project folders (e.g., `5_April_2026 New folder/v1`, `/v2`, `/v3`), which hold iterative documentation, scanned reference materials, and temporary working files

## Purpose

- Serve as a central, versioned record of project drafts and finalized documentation
- Facilitate review, cross-referencing, and progressive completion of official Mesob Center paperwork
- Archive material from various contributors and official bodies to track project evolution
